What is a Temporary Email Service?

A temporary email service provides disposable email addresses that you can use for a short period. These addresses allow you to receive emails, such as OTPs for website registrations or confirmations, without revealing your personal email. When to Use a Temporary Email

Temporary email services are ideal for various scenarios:

  • Signing up for a one-time service or trial that requires email verification.
  • Accessing content behind email registration walls, such as eBooks or webinars.
  • Testing apps or websites without committing your personal email.
  • Protecting your identity when interacting with potentially risky platforms.

However, for sensitive accounts like banking or long-term subscriptions, we recommend using a secure, personal email address.
