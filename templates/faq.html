ZyMail FAQ

What is ZyMail Temp Mail?

ZyMail Temp Mail is a free temporary email service that provides disposable email addresses for receiving emails without using your personal email. It's ideal for OTP verification, website registrations, and protecting your privacy online.

Is ZyMail Temp Mail really free?

Yes, ZyMail offers its temp mail service completely free with no hidden costs. All features, including email generation and inbox access, are available without charge.

How long does a temporary email last?

Your temporary email remains active until you manually delete it or generate a new one. Some services may automatically expire inactive addresses after a period, typically several hours to a few days.

Can I send emails from a temp mail address?

No, ZyMail's temp mail service is designed for receiving emails only. This ensures security and prevents misuse of the service. Use a regular email service for sending emails.

Why should I use a temp mail service?

Temp mail protects your privacy by keeping your real email address hidden, prevents spam in your primary inbox, and provides quick access for email verifications without registration. It's perfect for temporary sign-ups and OTPs.

What if a service blocks my temp mail address?

Some services block known temp mail domains. If this happens, generate a new temp mail address with ZyMail, as we offer multiple domain options. Alternatively, you may need to use a different temp mail service or your personal email.

Is ZyMail secure for receiving sensitive emails?

ZyMail is designed for temporary, non-sensitive use cases like OTPs and sign-ups. For sensitive accounts or password recovery, we recommend using a secure, personal email service to ensure maximum security.

How does the auto-refresh feature work?

ZyMail's inbox automatically checks for new messages every 5 seconds, ensuring you receive emails in real-time. You can also manually refresh the inbox using the refresh button for immediate updates.

Can I access ZyMail on my mobile device?

Yes, ZyMail is fully responsive and works seamlessly on desktops, tablets, and smartphones, providing a consistent experience across all devices.

Who created ZyMail?
