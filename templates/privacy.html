1. Information We Collect

ZyMail is designed to prioritize your privacy by minimizing data collection. We collect only the information necessary to provide our temporary email service:

  • Temporary Email Data: When you generate a temporary email address, we store the emails received by that address for a limited period. These emails are automatically deleted after a short duration (typically hours to a few days) or when you manually delete them.

The information we collect is used solely to operate and improve ZyMail’s temporary email service:

  • Service Delivery: To generate temporary email addresses and deliver emails to your temporary inbox.

  • Security: To detect and prevent abuse, fraud, or unauthorized access to our service.

We use your data to operate and improve ZyMail’s temporary email service, and to display relevant advertisements through third-party partners like Google AdSense. This helps us keep the service free for all users.

3. Data Storage and Retention

ZyMail is designed to be temporary and privacy-focused:

  • Temporary Emails: Emails received in your temporary inbox are stored for a short period (typically hours to a few days) and are automatically deleted thereafter. You can also manually delete emails or generate a new address to clear the inbox.
